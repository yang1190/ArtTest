Scene Context

Recreate the solitude and steadfastness of the Tang Dynasty frontier outpost of Shazhou. Through the extreme contrast of "cold and warmth" — the desert under moonlight and the campfire in the outpost — build an immersive night scene filled with narrative tension.

Design Language

The core visual leading line in the original concept art directs from the dark foreground roof to the bright midground campfire. To emphasize this, I used volumetric fog in UE5 to enhance atmospheric perspective, darkening the surrounding cool-toned areas and ensuring the visual focus remains on the warm center.

For architectural materials, I studied the rammed earth wall textures around Dunhuang and created an intelligent material with wind erosion layers in UE5 master materials.

Reflections

The biggest challenge was in controlling the details in the shadows. The concept art's background was very dark, but in a 3D scene, pure black would lose the sense of volume. I fine-tuned the intensity of Skylight and added a slight rim light to ensure that the distant architectural structures remained distinguishable while maintaining the atmosphere of the night scene. This was an immersive exploration of Tang Dynasty frontier aesthetics.
